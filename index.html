Richard Spence

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Arizona. My research interests include analysis of algorithms, graph theory, and theory of computation. I received a B.S. in Mathematics with Computer Science (Course 18C) from MIT in 2016. My advisor is Stephen Kobourov.
