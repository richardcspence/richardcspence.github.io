Richard Spence

I completed my Ph.D. from the Department of Computer Science at the University of Arizona, under Stephen Kobourov. My research interests include analysis of algorithms, graph theory, and theory of computation. I received the B.S. in Mathematics with Computer Science (Course 18C) from MIT in 2016. As of March 2022, I will be starting a postdoc at Ben-Gurion University of the Negev under Michael Elkin and Ofer Neiman. My hobbies include Scrabble (cross-tables profile), other board/video games, and bowling.
