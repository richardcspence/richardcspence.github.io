Richard Spence

I am a researcher in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Arizona, working under Stephen Kobourov. My research interests include analysis of algorithms, graph theory, and theory of computation. I received the B.S. in Mathematics with Computer Science (Course 18C) from MIT in 2016, and the M.S. and Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of Arizona. As of 2022, I will be starting a postdoc at Ben-Gurion University of the Negev under Michael Elkin and Ofer Neiman. My hobbies include Scrabble (cross-tables profile), other board/video games, and bowling.
